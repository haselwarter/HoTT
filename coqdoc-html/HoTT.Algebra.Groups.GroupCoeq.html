Coequalizers of group homomorphisms

Definition GroupCoeq {A B : Group} (f g : A $-> B) : Group.
Proof.
  rapply (AmalgamatedFreeProduct (FreeProduct A A) A B).
  1,2: apply FreeProduct_rec.
  + exact grp_homo_id.
  + exact grp_homo_id.
  + exact f.
  + exact g.
Defined.
